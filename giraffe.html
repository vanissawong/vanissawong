One of my best friends that I knew from middle school gave it to me for my birthday a long time ago. It’s the only stuffed animal I brought with me when moving to the US. It stays on my bed always. I don’t really interact with it as much anymore but the fact that it’s here with me is a reminder of home and my friends to me. Meeting up with the friend group I had at the time is still the first thing I do during the summer, when I do get home. We went though a tough time together in a intense education system, so lot of who I am, skills, hobbies, personality also came from those friends. It’s important to me to have a reminder to not forget who I am before being exposed to the new environment that is the US.
