Every time I left the house for an extended period of time (usually a semester of college), my parents would give me a bunch of red envelopes. At first I thought it was just an elaborate gesture for giving me money, but they would also give me money in addition to these envelopes so that wasn't it. I guess they just wanted to wish me good luck. During college I would always keep these envelopes in the front pocket of my backpack. I'd leave these envelopes unopened and intact because they reminded me of my parents and thus of home. This photo is of the red envelopes they gave to me for the fall semester.
