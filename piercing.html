I got my first piercing while I was in highschool and during that time it was very popular to get piercings. I wanted something that I could carry with me and leave an impression on my physical self wherever I go. Since I travel so much and home is such a fluid concept it’s nice to have something tangible to hold on. Unlike a normal piercing it’s also permanently placed on my ear. Unless I go to the store to get it professionally removed it’s not easy to take off. It’s also very hidden which I like because it’s in a place no one can really see since my hair hides it. It’s very small and unnoticeable but it’s holds a special place in my heart.
