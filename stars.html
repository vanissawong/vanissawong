One of the few things that remind me of home are the stars I see when I look up at the night sky. As I wait for my parents to unlock our front door, I often find myself looking up at the stars. It’s become a habit that I’ve taken for granted over the years, and it was only when I came home from college for the first time that I really fully noticed them. My night sky might not be as full or bright as it is in some other parts of the world, but I like to believe it’s a lot more unique than it may seem. Whenever I come home and look up, I instantly take in the stars and realize that I haven’t seen the same sky in a while; it’s the sky I grew up under for most of my life, the sky that never seems to change, and the sky that never fails to remind me of home.
