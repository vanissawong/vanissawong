When I first came to college, I only brought 2 suitcases and a backpack so I only brought necessities. Over time, a lot of the stuff I've accumulated was purchased here. I also didn't start making coffee by myself for a while because I was living in dorms and had unlimited access to the cafeteria but now I live off-campus. The process of making coffee in the morning reminds me a lot of home because my family universally loves coffee and mornings with my parents are the only time we're at peace. No one fights, no one bickers.
