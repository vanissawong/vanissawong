My grandmother got me my teddy bear and it was immediately my favorite thing. When I was really young I wouldn’t be able to sleep without it. My mom also used to “fill it up with her love” whenever she had to go away for a trip so I wouldn’t miss her too much. I’ve always kept it in my room somewhere and it always makes me so happy to see it. It is definitely the physical representation for my family’s love will always be my home away from home.
