I received these slippers at the beginning of my senior year of college. It reminds me of home because my mom would always be wearing these inside the house and would say she needs them when doing chores and running errands. It always gave me joy seeing her in these and just cooking breakfast or folding laundry and I would always love to wear them as well and help her because I wanted to be like her.
