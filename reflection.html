To me a home isn't tangible. Its a a state of mind that a person creates for themselves. Since a sense of home can metamorphose over time I dont think I can choose a singular physical object that remind me of home because that would seemingly implicate a fixed point or idea of home for me. Thats why I chose reflection. For I can seemingly only conjure/understand a sense of home through self reflection. Without reflection a home is seemingly sublime, for if u dont think abt what home means to you how can you ever establish your own sense of home. Edit: Upon reflection I think I feel most at home when I feel the freedom of individuality.
